Hi. Thanks for the introduction, Aimee.
I'm excited to be here tonight, and to give a few of my thoughts about how the Rust language relates to various blockchain projects.
For those that don't know her, Aimee Zhu, our event organizer, is the head of developer relations for the Nervos network, a blockchain written in Rust.
She has been working to unite multiple Rust blockchain communities, and to raise their visibility in the wider Rust community.
This is a goal that interests me a lot, as blockchain projects have turned out to be some of the most enthusiastic adopters of — contributors to — Rust, and I hope for this to be better appreciated.
